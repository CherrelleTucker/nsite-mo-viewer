## Getting Started

### First Time Access

1. Navigate to the MO Viewer URL (provided by your team lead)
2. Enter your NASA email address
3. Enter the team passphrase (ask your team lead if you don't have it)
4. Click **Sign In**

Your session lasts 6 hours. After that, you'll need to sign in again.

### Navigation

MO Viewer has seven main pages, accessible from the navigation bar at the top:

| Page | Icon | Purpose |
|------|------|---------|
| **Implementation** | construction | Solution lifecycle tracking, milestones, documents |
| **SEP** | handshake | Stakeholder engagement pipeline and agency relationships |
| **Comms** | campaign | Story pipeline, events, communications coverage |
| **Team** | groups | Team info, actions, kudos, meetings, parking lot |
| **Contacts** | contacts | Stakeholder contact directory and search |
| **Reports** | assessment | Analytics, exports, and specialized reports |
| **Schedule** | calendar_month | Milestone timeline and Gantt chart views |

**Tip:** The page you're on is highlighted in the navigation bar.

## Quick Reference FAQ

### General Tasks

#### How do I find information about a specific solution?

**Quick answer:** Use **Ctrl+K** (or Cmd+K on Mac) to open Global Search, then type the solution name.

**Alternative:** Go to **Implementation** â†’ Use the **Solution picker** dropdown â†’ Select your solution â†’ Click the solution card to see details.

#### How do I search for a contact?

**Quick answer:** Go to **Contacts** â†’ Type a name, email, or organization in the search box.

**With filters:** Use the dropdowns to filter by Department, Solution, Role, or Year before searching.

---

#### How do I export data to a spreadsheet?

Most pages have an **Export** button (download icon) that creates a CSV file:

- **Contacts:** Export filtered contact list
- **Schedule:** Export filtered milestones
- **Reports:** Export any report to CSV or create a Google Sheet

Look for **view toggle buttons** near the top of each page. For example:

- **SEP:** Dashboard | Solutions | Agencies
- **Comms:** Pipeline | Events | Coverage | Calendar | Priorities | Messages | Tools
- **Team:** Overview | Actions | Kudos | Meetings | Documents | Parking Lot

Click the button for the view you want.

## Feature Walkthroughs

### Global Search

**What it does:** Searches across all solutions, contacts, and actions from anywhere in the app.

**How to use it:**

1. Press **Ctrl+K** (Windows) or **Cmd+K** (Mac) from any page
2. A search overlay appears
3. Start typing - results appear instantly
4. Results are grouped by type (Solutions, Contacts, Actions)
5. Click a result to navigate directly to it

**Tips:**
- Search finds partial matches (typing "HLS" finds "HLS", "HLS-LL", etc.)
- Results are de-duplicated automatically
- The search stops early once it has enough relevant results for speed

> **Screenshot location:** Global search overlay with results grouped by type

---

### Solution Cards

**What it does:** Provides a comprehensive view of any solution's status, updates, stakeholders, and documents in one modal.

**How to use it:**

1. Go to **Implementation**
2. Click any solution card in the grid

**What you'll see:**

| Section | Information |
|---------|-------------|
| **Header** | Solution name, lifecycle phase badge |
| **Recent Updates** | Last 10 updates from meeting notes with dates and sources |
| **Stakeholders** | Contact counts by role (Primary SME, Secondary SME, etc.) with email links |
| **Milestone Status** | ATP, F2I, ORR, Closeout dates and status indicators |
| **Document Administration** | Grid showing completion status of Project Plan, Science SOW, IPA, ICD, TTA |
| **Upcoming Milestones** | Next scheduled milestones with dates |

**Actions available:**
- **Email Stakeholders** - Opens email draft with all stakeholder emails
- **Share File** - Upload a file to the team's Drive folder
- **Edit** - Update solution information (if you have permissions)
- **View All Updates** - Go to Historical Updates report for this solution

> **Screenshot location:** Annotated solution card modal highlighting each section

### Agency Heat Mapping

**What it does:** Visualizes engagement levels across federal agencies to identify relationship gaps.

**How to access:**

1. Go to **SEP**
2. Click **Agencies** view

**How to read the heat map:**

| Color | Indicator | Meaning |
|-------|-----------|---------|
| Red | Hot | 4+ engagements in tracking period |
| Orange | Warm | 1-3 engagements in tracking period |
| Blue/Gray | Cold | 0 engagements - needs attention |

**How to use it:**

1. **Scan the Tree**: Look for cold (gray/blue) agencies that need outreach
2. **Click an Agency**: Right panel shows all contacts and engagement history
3. **Drill Down**: Sub-agencies inherit from parent or show their own activity
4. **Plan Outreach**: Use the contact list to identify who to reach out to

**Tips:**
- Parent agencies show aggregate engagement from all sub-agencies
- Click contacts in the detail panel to see their full profile
- Use this view before conferences to identify networking targets

> **Screenshot location:** Agency tree view with heat indicators and detail panel showing contacts

## Troubleshooting

### "I can't sign in"

- Verify you're using your NASA email address
- Check that you have the current team passphrase
- Contact your team lead to confirm you're on the whitelist

### "A page shows no data"

- Check your filters - you may have restrictive filters applied
- Click "Clear All" on the filter bar
- Try refreshing the page

### "The solution I need isn't showing"

- In Implementation, check the **Solution picker** dropdown
- Make sure "Default Solutions" vs "All Solutions" is set correctly
- Use Global Search (Ctrl+K) to find it directly

### "My changes aren't saving"

- Wait for the save confirmation toast message
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing and making the change again
- If persistent, contact your team lead

### "The data seems outdated"

- Data syncs automatically from meeting agendas weekly
- Updates appear after Internal Planning and SEP meetings
- For urgent data corrections, contact the system administrator
